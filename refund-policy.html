Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 3, 2026

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase date.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, you must:

  • Request the refund within 14 days of purchase
  • Provide a brief explanation of why the product didn't meet your needs
  • Have made a good-faith effort to use the product

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us via the email provided with your purchase confirmation
  2. Include your order number and purchase date
  3. Briefly explain why you're requesting a refund

Refund Processing

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • The refund will be issued to your original payment method
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is processed

No Refunds After 14 Days

Unfortunately, we cannot process refunds for purchases made more than 14 days ago. We encourage you to review the product details carefully before purchasing and reach out if you have any questions.

Abuse Prevention

We reserve the right to refuse refunds if we detect abuse of this policy, including but not limited to:

  • Multiple refund requests from the same customer
  • Evidence that the product was fully used or copied before requesting a refund
  • Fraudulent or dishonest refund claims

Technical Issues

If you experience technical difficulties downloading or accessing your purchase:

  • Contact support first - we'll work to resolve the issue
  • Technical issues do not require a refund request
  • We'll ensure you receive your product properly

Questions About Refunds

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us before making a purchase. We're happy to answer any questions to help you make an informed decision.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
